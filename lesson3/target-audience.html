    • Target Audience Description

    The target audience is mainly adults who are either new to the area, recently moved into the area, or are passing through and want to learn more. It is also mainly focused on those who live in the city to help create a better community of friendship between the community members. It is here to uplift and support the community and anyone who is passing through and interested.
